Dark Matter/Energy

Dark matter is the matter formed from Dark Energy. Now, let us see what is Dark Energy. Dark energy is a theoretical form of energy postulated to act in opposition to gravity and to occupy the entire universe, accounting for most of the energy in it and causing its expansion to accelerate.
