Universe

Where does everything we know about space exist? You guessed it, its the Universe. Now lets learn about it...

How did it start?

The best known and supported theory for the universe is "The Big Bang". In this theory it says : At first the universe was a small object minute (smaller) than an atom. It had infinite matter but then suddenly it bursted with a huge Big Bang. Matter flung everywhere and from that moment on till present day the Universe keeps expanding in increasing pace day by day.

Why name it Universe?

The word Universe has 2 parts : uni- meaning 1 and verse meaning realm.
So, the word universe means "1 realm". This was based on Einstein's theory on having parallel universes and that our universe is only one many realms.

Can we reach its edge?

The direct answer to this question is No.However I am going to explain why it is a no. As you already know in the big bang there was a huge burst. And as time went on the Universe kept expanding at excelerating speeds. As it started to cool down new elements started forming, which then combined becoming different celestial bodies. The size of the universe and number of new bodies kept increasing. While, some bodies went to their demise they did not take spaces of the universe with them. Thus the universe never decreased in size and kept increasing in size and speed.
