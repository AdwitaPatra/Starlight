Eclipses

There are two eclipses as you know. Now, let's take a look at how they are formed and clear some common, yet understandable doubts.....

Lunar Eclipse

I am writing about this first as there is a huge misconception about this one. A Lunar Eclipse occurs when the Sun,Earth and Moon. There are extremely high chances of a blood moon but now here comes the misconseption. It does not mean that a bloodmoon = Lunar Eclipse. To put it simply, when the red colour fades in it is a normal blood moon and when it comes in and goes away like solar eclipse it is a lunar eclipse.

Solar Eclipse

Solar Eclipses are formed when the Sun, Earrth and Moon come in one line with Moon being in the center. You should NEVER look at a Solar eclipse with your naked eyes.
